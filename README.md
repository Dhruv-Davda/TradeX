# Trade Management App

The **Trade Management App** is a web-based application designed for gold and silver merchants to efficiently manage their trades. It helps merchants streamline operations by tracking trades, managing stock levels, calculating profit and loss (P&L), and providing insightful analytics.

---

## Features

### 1. Trade Management
- Add, edit, and delete trades for gold and silver.
- Track trade details such as quantity, price, and type (buy/sell/settlements).
- Maintain a history of all trades for future reference.

### 2. Stock Management
- Automatically update stock levels based on trades.
- View current stock levels for gold and silver in real-time.

### 3. Profit & Loss Calculation
- Calculate P&L for each trade.
- View cumulative P&L for a specific time period.

### 4. Analytics Dashboard
- Visualize trade data with charts and graphs.
- Analyze trends in trading activity, stock levels, and P&L over time.

### 5. Merchants Page
- Track dues and owes (khata) for each merchant.
- Maintain a clear ledger of outstanding balances and payments.
- View merchant-wise trade and payment history.
